# Performing an Instant Scan

This document outlines the steps to initiate an Instant Scan for your project in **CyberWacht**.

## Steps to Perform an Instant Scan

1. **Navigate to the Project Overview Page**  
   Click on the project name in the left-hand sidebar to access its overview.

2. **Open the Scan Tab**

   - On the project overview page, locate and click the **Scan** tab.
   - Click the **+Scan Now** button to open the modal for selecting scanners.

3. **Select Scan Type(s)**  
   Choose the desired scan type(s) from the available options:

   - **Select a Specific Scanner:** Focus the scan on particular areas of your project (e.g., code analysis, dependency checks).
   - **Choose Select All:** Perform a comprehensive scan of all domains and repositories using all available scanners.

4. **Start the Scan**  
   After selecting the scan type(s), click the **Start Scan** button.

5. **Monitor the Scan Progress**  
   View the scan’s progress in real-time on the **Scans** page.  
   Once completed, the results will be displayed in the **Findings** section.

## Post-Scan Actions

- **Review the Report:** Analyze the vulnerabilities detected, categorized by severity and type.
- **Take Action:** Use the remediation steps provided in the report to fix vulnerabilities immediately.
- **Re-run if Needed:** Trigger another scan after making changes to validate fixes.

With CyberWacht’s Instant Scan, you can quickly analyze your project’s security posture and address vulnerabilities in real-time.
