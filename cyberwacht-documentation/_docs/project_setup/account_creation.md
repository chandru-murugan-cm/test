### Introduction:

This document outlines the steps involved in creating a new user account on the CyberWacht platform.

### Steps:

- **Navigate to the Registration Page:**

  - Open your web browser and go to the CyberWacht registration page( **[URL](https://cyberwacht.decentralcode.tech/)**)

- **Enter User Information:**

  - **First Name:** Enter your first name.
  - **Last Name:** Enter your last name.
  - **Email:** Provide a valid email address.
  - **Organization Name:** Enter the name of your organization.

- **Set Your Password:**

  - **Password:** Choose a strong password with the following requirements:
    - Minimum length of 8 characters.
    - At least one uppercase letter.
    - At least one lowercase letter.
    - At least one number or symbol.
  - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter the password to ensure accuracy.

- **Review and Submit:**
  - **Review** all the information you've entered to confirm its accuracy.
  - Once satisfied, click the **Register** button to submit your account creation request.

### Additional Notes:

- Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the process.
- If you encounter any issues, please reach out to CyberWacht support at [support email address].
- For detailed usage instructions, refer to the CyberWacht User Guide.
