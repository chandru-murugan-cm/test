# License & SBOM (Software Bill of Materials)

The License & SBOM page in CyberWacht is a dedicated section designed to review findings related to licenses and the Software Bill of Materials (SBOM). This page ensures compliance and transparency by offering detailed insights into open-source components, licenses, and associated potential risks.

## Key Features

### License Compliance

- **Detailed License Information**: View comprehensive details about open-source licenses used in your project.
- **Compliance Checks**: Ensure all licenses comply with your organization’s policies and legal standards.
- **Risk Mitigation**: Identify and address potential legal risks associated with license usage.

### SBOM Insights

- **Dependency Management**: Review all open-source components and their dependencies.
- **Transparency**: Gain a clear understanding of the software’s composition, enabling proactive risk management.
- **Versioning and Updates**: Track component versions to ensure up-to-date usage and identify deprecated or vulnerable components.

## Benefits

- **Enhanced Governance**: Improve open-source software governance through detailed tracking and reporting.
- **Risk Reduction**: Mitigate legal and security risks by proactively identifying non-compliant or risky components.
- **Streamlined Audits**: Simplify audit processes by maintaining an accessible and organized SBOM.

## Usage Scenarios

- **Legal Audits**: Use the License & SBOM page to prepare for legal or compliance audits.
- **Risk Assessments**: Conduct thorough evaluations of potential risks associated with open-source usage.
- **Development Oversight**: Provide developers with clear guidelines and insights to ensure compliant and secure code development.

## How It Works

1. **Data Collection**: Automatically scan the project to gather information on open-source components and their licenses.
2. **Analysis**: Evaluate collected data against compliance criteria and organizational policies.
3. **Reporting**: Generate detailed reports summarizing findings, risks, and actionable recommendations.

## Conclusion

The License & SBOM page in CyberWacht is an essential tool for organizations relying on open-source software. It not only ensures compliance and mitigates risks but also enhances transparency and governance, making it an invaluable resource for maintaining software integrity.
