# Scheduler for Scans

**CyberWacht** includes a powerful scheduling feature that allows users to automate security scans of their repositories at regular intervals. This feature ensures consistent monitoring of your codebase for vulnerabilities without requiring manual intervention, helping to maintain a robust security posture.

## Scheduling Options

CyberWacht offers flexible scheduling options to fit your specific needs:

### Daily Scans

- Automate scans to run every day, ensuring timely detection of vulnerabilities.
- Ideal for projects with frequent updates or deployments where security is critical.

### Weekly Scans

- Set up weekly scans to maintain a consistent overview of your security status.
- Best suited for projects with moderate activity, balancing security with operational efficiency.

### Monthly Scans

- Schedule scans on a monthly basis for projects with less frequent changes.
- Suitable for stable codebases or those in maintenance mode.

## Key Features

### Customizable Time Slots

- Select specific days and times for scans to run, ensuring minimal disruption to your workflow.
- Align scan schedules with team availability or deployment cycles.

### Scalability

- Schedule scans for multiple repositories at once, simplifying the management of large projects.
- Tailor the frequency of scans per repository based on their activity level and criticality.

### Audit and Compliance Tracking

- Maintain a log of scheduled and completed scans for compliance purposes.
- Ensure your project adheres to organizational or regulatory requirements with automated documentation.
