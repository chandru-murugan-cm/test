# Setting Up Scheduled Scans in CyberWacht

This document outlines the steps to set up scheduled scans for your project in CyberWacht.

---

## Prerequisites

- A project with a repository or domain added.

---

## Steps to Schedule Scans

### 1. Navigate to the Project Overview Page

Click on the project name in the left-hand sidebar.

### 2. Open the Schedule Scan Tab

On the project overview page, locate and click the **Schedule** tab.

### 3. Select Scan Type(s)

Choose the desired scan type(s) from the available options:

- **Select a Specific Scanner**: Focus the scan on particular aspects of your project.
- **Choose Select All**: Scan all domains and repositories with all available scanners.

### 4. Configure the Scan Schedule

Choose the desired frequency for the scans:

- **Daily Scans**: Set the specific time for the scan to run.
- **Weekly or Monthly Scans**: Select the specific days of the week or month for the scans to run.

### 5. Submit the Schedule

After configuring the scan schedule, click the **"Submit"** button to initiate the scheduled scans.

---

## Additional Tips

### Customize Scan Settings

- Adjust scan settings as needed, such as selecting specific branches to scan or configuring additional scan parameters.

### Monitor Scan Results

- Regularly review scan results to identify and address any vulnerabilities.

### Adjust the Schedule as Needed

- Modify the scan frequency and scan types based on the evolving needs of your project.

### Leverage CyberWacht's Insights

- Use the detailed reports and recommendations provided by CyberWacht to enhance your application's security posture.
